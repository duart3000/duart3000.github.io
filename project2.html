anPARK

anPARK is a small architecture atelier that collaborates with people and companies in South Korea, through different projects and educational workshops. Their prime focus is to work closely with the client to give a personalized experience and to make every project unique and distinctive.

The atelier works inside out to understand the whole on a personal level with the client. Thus, the logo reflects those concepts, creating a visual condensation of the ideas of construction, human warmth, and comprehension.
