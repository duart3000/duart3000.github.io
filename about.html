About me

Born in 87', a lover of 80's music, games and color. A designer/musician who is passionate about both areas, strives to keep improving and developing his skills. Passionate about Front-End, UX/UI and 3D Graphics.

Currently works as a musical director for two musical shows and as a guitar teacher. Has some experience with modeling assets for game development, rigging and 3D animation.

Major in Graphic Design - FBAUP, Porto University of Fine Arts.
