Capsule Weather

The CW4 is in a personal capsule weather constructed from a new resistant and moldable material formed by nano-particles. The number 4 refers to the four seasons of the year. It combines modern mechanisms and controls, allowing the choice of the surrounding environment. Since climate change and/or season, as well as the desired location in the world, each capsule can only be used individually; the user has full control of it and can also add extras to his capsule.

The CW4 holds a technology capable of teleporting the user to a supersonic speed: in just a few seconds it's possible to travel to another location on the planet. The way this capsule presents itself , internally and externally, can be customized to a personal taste with the possibility of being imperceptible in the environment. Created by innovative Nanotech Industry, in its original state, the CW4 has a complex interior due the multiple commands that enable a high variety of choices.
